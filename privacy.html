Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Information we collect

We may collect the name, contact details and contribution reference voluntarily shared by visitors for festival coordination and acknowledgement.

Information is used only to coordinate Sri Mahakaala Vinayaka Samithi activities, respond to enquiries, acknowledge contributions and maintain community records.

UPI payments are completed in the visitor’s chosen payment app. This website does not collect card details, UPI PINs or bank login credentials.

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only where reasonably necessary for festival administration or when required by law.
