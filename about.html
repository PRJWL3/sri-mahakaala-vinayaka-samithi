About the Samithi

Sri Mahakaala Vinayaka Samithi brings devotees, families and neighbours together to celebrate Vinayaka Chavithi with devotion, cultural spirit and community care.

Our purpose

We organise puja, aarti, prasad distribution, cultural activities and an eco-conscious visarjan. Contributions are used for festival arrangements and community activities connected with the celebration.

Our commitment

We aim to make every celebration welcoming, respectful and transparent. We value participation from every generation and invite the community to help shape the programme through the activity poll.
